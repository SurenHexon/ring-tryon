How to use

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Hold your hand open in front of the camera, palm facing you.
  3. The ring snaps onto your selected finger automatically.
  4. Use Ring Size slider to adjust the fit.
  5. Switch fingers using the buttons top-left.
  6. Upload your own .glb ring model for a custom preview.

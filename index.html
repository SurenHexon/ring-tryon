How to use

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Hold your hand open in front of the camera, palm facing you.
  3. The ring will snap onto your selected finger automatically.
  4. Use Ring Size slider to adjust fit.
  5. Change which finger to try with the finger buttons.
  6. Upload your own .glb ring model for a custom preview.
